# Customer Journey Map

## Phase 1: Aware
- **Trigger Event**: Developer or AI/prompt engineer encounters issues with model performance variability across different LLM providers.
- **Friction Points**: Lack of standardized metrics for comparing LLM providers, difficulty in finding reliable performance data.
- **User Emotions**: Frustration, confusion, uncertainty.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Provide clear, concise comparisons of LLM performance metrics in a digestible format.
- **Metric**: Number of unique visitors to the product landing page.

## Phase 2: Consider
- **Trigger Event**: Developer or AI/prompt engineer searches for solutions to compare LLM provider performance.
- **Friction Points**: Overwhelming number of options, lack of transparency in performance claims, difficulty in understanding the value proposition.
- **User Emotions**: Overwhelmed, skeptical, hopeful.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Offer free comparative reports, case studies, and testimonials from other developers.
- **Metric**: Conversion rate from landing page to sign-up.

## Phase 3: Try
- **Trigger Event**: Developer or AI/prompt engineer signs up for a free trial or demo of the platform.
- **Friction Points**: Complex onboarding process, lack of clear instructions, technical issues during setup.
- **User Emotions**: Anxious, curious, eager.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Provide a seamless onboarding experience with guided tutorials and instant support.
- **Metric**: Number of completed sign-ups and successful trial activations.

## Phase 4: Adopt
- **Trigger Event**: Developer or AI/prompt engineer finds the platform useful and decides to subscribe.
- **Friction Points**: Pricing concerns, integration challenges with existing workflows, lack of advanced features.
- **User Emotions**: Satisfied, confident, committed.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Offer flexible pricing plans, robust API documentation, and personalized support.
- **Metric**: Conversion rate from trial to paid subscription.

## Phase 5: Expand
- **Trigger Event**: Developer or AI/prompt engineer realizes the platform's full potential and seeks to expand usage.
- **Friction Points**: Limited scalability, lack of advanced analytics, difficulty in onboarding team members.
- **User Emotions**: Excited, ambitious, loyal.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Provide enterprise-level features, team collaboration tools, and regular updates on new functionalities.
- **Metric**: Number of additional team members added, usage frequency, and revenue from upsells.
