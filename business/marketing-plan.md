# Marketing Plan
## Positioning
1. **Primary Positioning**: "Model-Metrics: The Industry Standard for Comparative LLM Performance Analysis"
2. **Alternative 1**: "LLM Performance Tracker: Your One-Stop Shop for Model Evaluation"
3. **Alternative 2**: "Model-Metrics: Unlocking the Full Potential of LLMs with Data-Driven Insights"
4. **Alternative 3**: "The LLM Performance Benchmark: Where Developers Meet Data-Driven Decision-Making"

## ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)
### Persona 1: Alex Chen, AI Engineer
- **Daily Job**: Lead AI Engineer at a mid-sized tech firm, responsible for evaluating and implementing LLMs for various projects.
- **Budget**: $5,000 - $10,000 per year for AI-related tools and services.
- **Pain Points**: Difficulty in comparing LLM performance across different providers, limited visibility into model behavior.

### Persona 2: Maya Patel, Prompt Engineer
- **Daily Job**: Prompt Engineer at a large e-commerce company, responsible for designing and optimizing prompts for LLMs.
- **Budget**: $2,000 - $5,000 per year for AI-related tools and services.
- **Pain Points**: Limited ability to track and analyze LLM performance, difficulty in identifying areas for prompt optimization.

### Persona 3: Ethan Kim, DevOps Manager
- **Daily Job**: DevOps Manager at a large financial institution, responsible for ensuring the reliability and performance of LLM-based systems.
- **Budget**: $10,000 - $20,000 per year for AI-related tools and services.
- **Pain Points**: Difficulty in monitoring and troubleshooting LLM performance, limited visibility into model behavior.

## Channels
1. **Top Channel 1: LinkedIn Ads** (CAC estimate: $50)
- Target audience: AI engineers, prompt engineers, and DevOps managers at mid-sized to large tech firms.
- Ad creative: Showcase the benefits of Model-Metrics in a visually appealing and concise manner.

2. **Top Channel 2: Email Marketing** (CAC estimate: $20)
- Target audience: Existing contacts and subscribers to the Axentx newsletter.
- Email content: Highlight the unique value proposition of Model-Metrics and provide a free trial or demo.

3. **Top Channel 3: Content Marketing** (CAC estimate: $30)
- Target audience: AI and machine learning enthusiasts, developers, and engineers.
- Content types: Blog posts, case studies, and whitepapers that showcase the benefits and use cases of Model-Metrics.

## Content Cadence
Week 1-4: Establish a consistent content publishing schedule, focusing on blog posts and social media updates.
Week 5-8: Launch a series of case studies and whitepapers highlighting the benefits and use cases of Model-Metrics.
Week 9-12: Host a webinar or online event to showcase the features and capabilities of Model-Metrics.

## Launch Milestones
- **D-30 (30 days before launch)**: Finalize the marketing plan, content calendar, and launch preparations.
- **D-0 (launch day)**: Launch the Model-Metrics platform, announce the launch on social media and email marketing channels.
- **D+30 (30 days after launch)**: Analyze the initial results, gather feedback from customers and users.
- **D+90 (90 days after launch)**: Review the progress, adjust the marketing strategy as needed, and plan for future growth.

## Success Metrics
- **DAU (Daily Active Users)**: 100 users within the first 30 days, 500 users within the first 90 days.
- **$MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue)**: $5,000 within the first 30 days, $20,000 within the first 90 days.
