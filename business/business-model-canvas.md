# Business Model Canvas
## Customer Segments
* **Primary Customers**: AI/prompt engineers, developers, and data scientists working with Large Language Models (LLMs)
* **Secondary Customers**: LLM providers, researchers, and academics interested in model performance evaluation
* **Target Market Size**: Estimated 10,000+ developers and AI engineers in the LLM space, with a growth rate of 20% YoY
* **Geographic Reach**: Global, with a focus on North America, Europe, and Asia-Pacific regions
* **Demographics**: Tech-savvy individuals with a strong background in AI, machine learning, and software development

## Value Propositions
* **Unique Selling Point (USP)**: Model-metrics provides a comprehensive platform for comparative analysis and tracking of LLM provider performance
* **Key Benefits**:
	+ Enables developers to test and evaluate model behavior side-by-side
	+ Provides actionable insights for LLM provider selection and optimization
	+ Facilitates collaboration and knowledge sharing among developers and researchers
* **Pain Point Addressed**: The lack of a dedicated platform for LLM performance evaluation and comparison
* **Competitive Advantage**: Model-metrics' proprietary algorithms and data visualization tools provide a unique value proposition in the market

## Channels
* **Primary Channels**: Online marketing, social media, and content marketing to reach the target audience
* **Secondary Channels**: Partnerships with LLM providers, research institutions, and industry events to expand reach
* **Marketing Budget**: $50,000 (USD) / 1,800,000 (THB) for the first quarter, with a growth rate of 20% QoQ
* **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**: Estimated $100 (USD) / 3,600 (THB) per customer
* **Conversion Rate**: 2% from lead to paying customer

## Customer Relationships
* **Primary Relationship**: Developer-centric, with a focus on providing value through education and support
* **Secondary Relationship**: Partnerships with LLM providers to ensure accurate and up-to-date model performance data
* **Customer Support**: Email, chat, and community support for developers and researchers
* **Community Engagement**: Regular blog posts, webinars, and social media updates to foster a sense of community

## Revenue Streams
* **Primary Revenue Stream**: Subscription-based model, with tiered pricing for individuals and organizations
* **Secondary Revenue Stream**: Data licensing and partnerships with LLM providers
* **Revenue Growth Rate**: 30% YoY for the first three years, with a growth rate of 20% YoY thereafter
* **Average Revenue Per User (ARPU)**: $500 (USD) / 18,000 (THB) per year
* **Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)**: Estimated $2,000 (USD) / 72,000 (THB) per customer

## Key Resources
* **Proprietary Algorithms**: Model-metrics' core technology for LLM performance evaluation and comparison
* **Data Visualization Tools**: Custom-built tools for data analysis and visualization
* **Development Team**: Experienced developers and researchers with expertise in AI and machine learning
* **Marketing and Sales Team**: Dedicated team for customer acquisition and retention
* **Server and Infrastructure**: Scalable infrastructure for handling large datasets and user traffic

## Key Activities
* **Product Development**: Continuous improvement and expansion of the platform's features and capabilities
* **Marketing and Sales**: Customer acquisition and retention through online marketing, social media, and content marketing
* **Partnerships and Collaborations**: Partnerships with LLM providers, research institutions, and industry events to expand reach and credibility
* **Community Engagement**: Regular blog posts, webinars, and social media updates to foster a sense of community
* **Customer Support**: Email, chat, and community support for developers and researchers

## Key Partners
* **LLM Providers**: Partnerships with leading LLM providers to ensure accurate and up-to-date model performance data
* **Research Institutions**: Partnerships with research institutions to stay at the forefront of AI and machine learning research
* **Industry Events**: Participation in industry events to expand reach and credibility
* **Data Providers**: Partnerships with data providers to ensure access to high-quality data
* **Technology Partners**: Partnerships with technology companies to integrate with their platforms and services

## Cost Structure
* **Development Costs**: $100,000 (USD) / 3,600,000 (THB) per quarter for development and maintenance
* **Marketing and Sales Costs**: $50,000 (USD) / 1,800,000 (THB) per quarter for customer acquisition and retention
* **Server and Infrastructure Costs**: $20,000 (USD) / 720,000 (THB) per quarter for scalable infrastructure
* **Salaries and Benefits**: $150,000 (USD) / 5,400,000 (THB) per quarter for the development, marketing, and sales teams
* **Miscellaneous Costs**: $10,000 (USD) / 360,000 (THB) per quarter for office expenses, travel, and other miscellaneous costs
